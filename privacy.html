Privacy Policy

Introduction

Dunman App is the official app for Dunman High School, Singapore. “We” or “us” refers to the student council body as well as the technical ICs and teachers involved in this project. “The App” refers to the Dunman App excluding the individual functions within it. This Privacy Policy applies whenever you use the App or any of the functions within. We control the storage of your data but never enable functions to access them without your express consent. Any functions that require your data will initiate a prompt that ensures that only functions you trust can access your data. None of your personal information is sent by the App online, only the hash of your email is transmitted to provide persistent customized experience across all your devices.

Information we collect

We collect the following information:

- Your email

Only Dunman High Staff and Students are allowed to access this App. We ensure this by checking against the domain of your email (“xxx@dhs.sg”) to ensure that your email is a Dunman High School email address. This is collected when you first sign in and is stored persistently on your device locally.

- Your username

In order to provide better experiences for each cohort of students, we use your username to determine your current level. Your username may also be used for authorization by individual functions within the app. This is collected when your first sign in and is stored persistently on your device locally.

How do we collect your information?

Information is collected from your Google account and profile.

Use of information

- Special Wallpapers

Special wallpapers may be available for groups of users. We use a hashed version of your email to check against the Firebase database whether these wallpapers will be displayed.

- Feedback Collections

You may opt out of anonymity so that we can contact you better and through other more appropriate platforms such as email. However, all feedbacks default to being anonymous.

Communications

We do not contact via any other communication means.
